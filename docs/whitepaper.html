White Papers

We are in the process of creating an updated and combined white paper. In the meantime, here are the two white papers that outline the parts of what is now Scholar Nexus.

Neuromatch Open Publishing white paper

Our first white paper was written as "Neuromatch Open Publishing". It covers the idea of a separate, commonly owned "Foundation" (Scholar Nexus) that manages and runs the core database and builds shared software tools, and "Pillars" which represent different user facing views on this database with different but aligned philosophies of how we can reform publishing. It also discusses what will now be called "Oaktree", a minimal "Pillar" allowing maximal freedom to authors and readers to engage with the literature in the way they want, without restrictions.

Open white paper

The Open "Pillar" shares the Scholar Nexus foundation, but has an emphasis on structured and anonymous post-publication peer review with social incentives to participate.
